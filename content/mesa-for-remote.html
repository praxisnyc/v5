What is Mesa

Built upon a gesture library, Mesa provides an open-source AR environment for task-specific apps.

Created with community use in mind, Mesa recognizes and responds to simultaneous actions of multiple users.

Mesa is hardware-agnostic, allowing for comprehensive setup and calibration with easily found hardware: camera (input), projector (output) and a computer.

Remote Work with Mesa

Mesa brings a secure and synchronous whiteboard experience to collaborators across the world.

Simply put, users don't have to be in the same location to make use of Mesa.

Who is Praxis

Praxis is a not-for-profit design studio with a primary interest of delivering tools for the commons, mainly through open-source technologies, aiming to help marginalized communities.
